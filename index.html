Global street wars

Global Street Wars is a multiplayer online (MO) game experience for FPS team competition both casually and in organized tournament play. The gameplay is based on a capture the flag (CTF) competition where players use non-lethal paint balls and paint bombs to defend their base and attack the opponent in a timed play session. Developed using unity3D and Photon PUN.

Crazy Penguin Assault

Crazy Penguin Assault is a dynamic strategy game that shifts the popular franchise from traditional physics-based catapult mechanics to an engaging, turn-based combat system. Players command an army of courageous penguins in their fight to reclaim Arctic territories from an invading force of polar bears and panda bears. The game challenges players to utilize tactical planning and a variety of specialized penguin units to clear each level of enemies.

3D Rollercoaster Rush Underground

3D Rollercoaster Rush Underground challenges players to navigate a mine cart journey through subterranean environments including dark caves, ancient ruins, and lava-filled tunnels. The project successfully merged core simulation mechanics with fast-paced action and 3D graphics, designed to maximize player engagement through a simple, one-touch control scheme that demanded precise execution.

Tower Bloxx Revolution

Tower Bloxx Revolution is an innovative sequel to the award-winning Tower Bloxx series, transforming the core gameplay experience with a futuristic theme and novel mechanics. Set in the vibrant Sky City - 2699, the game merges the classic, addictive physics-based block-stacking with new, fast-paced game modes and strategic city-building elements. The objective is to build towering skyscrapers with precision timing to house space toons, ultimately becoming the richest architect of the future metropolis
